Andhra Pradesh

  • Area: Approximately 162,975 square kilometers.
  • Population: As per the 2011 census, the population was over 49.4 million people.
  • Capital: Amaravati is the capital city under development, with Visakhapatnam and Kurnool serving as executive and judicial capitals respectively.

  • Economy: The state's economy is driven by agriculture, industry, and services. Major crops include rice, tobacco, and cotton. It also has a significant IT industry presence and a growing industrial sector.
  • Literacy Rate: The literacy rate in Andhra Pradesh was around 67.41% as per the 2011 census.
  • Languages: Telugu is the official language, spoken by the majority, along with Urdu and English.
  • Tourism: The state is known for its rich cultural heritage, temples (e.g., Tirupati Temple), beaches (e.g., Visakhapatnam), and natural attractions like the Araku Valley and the Borra Caves.
